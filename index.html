Tales From The Wasteland

Tales From The Wasteland is an improvised theatrical experience set after the collapse of modern civilisation.


Inspired by a single audience suggestion, the cast creates a fully original story following the inhabitants of a struggling settlement trying to survive in a changed world.


No script.
No planning.
One story, told once.

The Company

Wasteland Improv brings together performers passionate about long-form storytelling, character-driven narratives, and theatrical improvisation.

Pete Leaman

Artistic Director

Bristol-based improviser, producer and creator of Tales From The Wasteland. Pete specialises in long-form narrative improvisation and immersive theatrical worlds.
